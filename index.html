I have a candidate degree as interaction designer from Aarhus School of Architecture with focus on HCI and new tech. As an interaction designer I have worked primarily as a project manager in innovation and transformation processes where I combine design thinking and traditional strategies to inspire new perspective and insights. My effort is grounded in user centred design, marked research and a visionary approach to new products and processes. The combination of HCI, design and IT development has provides me with a great understanding of the possibilities of bottom-up development of IT and open source. I have experience in entrepreneurship and the public sector and have applied my finding across a diverse landscape of network, strategies, processes of transformation, development of new materials and composite materials. I work cross disciplines with stakeholders, customers and designers to reveal the findings creating the best possible foundation for the future design and strategy.
